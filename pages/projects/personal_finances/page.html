Personal Finances Tracking Report
Optimize your personal financial management. This dashboard offers a comprehensive view of your finances by comparing monthly income, expenses, and utilities against your goals and budgets. It helps you track your financial health through detailed visualizations of your total balance in relation to your goals and budget.
Value Proposition
The Personal Finances Dashboard leverages Power BI's advanced capabilities to offer a thorough and interactive overview of your financial data. By comparing monthly figures against your goals and budgets, it provides valuable insights into your financial performance, helping you make informed decisions and better manage your finances.
Features
Income vs Goals
Compares your actual monthly income with your financial goals, helping you monitor your progress and identify shortfalls.
Expenses vs Budget
Tracks your actual monthly expenses against your budget to highlight areas of overspending and opportunities for adjustment.
Utilities vs Expected Utilities
Evaluates your actual utility costs each month compared to expected values, ensuring you remain within your planned limits.
Total Balance vs Expected Balance
Shows the comparison between your actual total balance and your expected balance, providing a snapshot of your overall financial health.
Monthly Comparisons
Provides a detailed comparison of income, expenses, and utilities on a monthly basis to facilitate thorough financial analysis.
Categorized Panels
Each financial aspect is organized into distinct categories for streamlined analysis and easy navigation.
Technologies Implemented
Power BI
Primary tool for data visualization, enabling the creation of interactive dashboards and comprehensive data analysis. Utilizes DAX for data management, facilitating informed decision-making.
